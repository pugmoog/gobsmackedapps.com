OUR MISSION


At Gobsmacked Apps, we believe technology should make everyday life simpler, more understandable, and a little more enjoyable. We build thoughtfully designed mobile apps that solve real problems, answer meaningful questions, and create moments worth sharing. Whether we’re helping someone make an important financial decision, better understand a complex topic, or simply enjoy a laugh with friends and family, our goal is to deliver practical tools that people genuinely enjoy using.

All of our apps do at least one of the following:

  • Simplify Hard Questions

    by transforming complicated topics into clear, intuitive experiences that anyone can understand. We believe valuable information shouldn’t require specialized knowledge to be useful.

  • Make Data Usable

    by getting data from APIs, calculations, and even users and turning it into an easy to read, intuitive format. Data has the greatest value when it is presented in a way that is accessible and actionable.

  • Bring People Together

    by creating experiences that encourage conversation, connection, and shared moments. Some of our apps are designed to solve serious problems, while others exist simply to spark laughter and strengthen relationships.
Every app we create is guided by a simple philosophy: if we can save someone time, reduce uncertainty, help them make a better decision, or create a memorable moment with the people around them, we’ve accomplished our mission.
